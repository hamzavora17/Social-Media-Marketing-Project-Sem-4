The Art of Slow Living: Why Your Home Needs Breathing Room

In a world that demands constant speed, our homes have become the ultimate sanctuary. Slow living isn't just a trend—it's a conscious choice to curate a space that invites pause, reflection, and genuine connection.

At FurniCo, we believe that furniture plays a pivotal role in this philosophy. It’s not just about filling a room; it’s about selecting pieces that ground you. From the tactile warmth of our solid oak frames to the deep, embracing comfort of our cloud sectionals, every design decision is made with your well-being in mind. We are moving towards a future where less is truly more, focusing on sustainable materials that age gracefully alongside you.

The Story Behind FurniCo

Meet the visionary dedicated to transforming how we live.

Hamza Vora, Founder & Lead Designer

Hamza's journey began in a small woodworking shop, where he discovered his passion for transforming raw timber into functional art. Frustrated by the "fast furniture" culture that prioritized trends over longevity, he founded FurniCo in 2020 with a simple mission: to create pieces that last a lifetime.

With a background in architectural design and a deep respect for sustainable materials, Hamza oversees every collection. He believes that great design should be accessible, ethical, and above all, comfortable. Today, FurniCo supports over 50 local artisans and sources 100% of its wood from certified sustainable forests.


Philosophy: "Furniture isn't just about filling a space. It's about creating the backdrop for your life's most cherished moments."
